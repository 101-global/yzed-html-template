Dear RESERVED,

Have you ever considered becoming a part of the new fashion movement called Democratic Fashion? If you ever wanted to communicate your product to your customers more effectively - then let us take a few minutes of your time.

Democratic Fashion is a practice that minimizes the gap between a designers’ work and the mass market, while considering fair economic and social trade off. Thus, by becoming a part of Democratic Fashion, your brand will be able to spread your messages to the Democratic Fashion Community, increasing brand awareness among our loyal community members.

YZED is one of the first pioneers in Democratic Fashion movement. We strongly believe that Democratic Fashion catalyses self-discovery, because our project originated from the desire to make fashion more accessible for the people. In 2019, we started from creating 3D models of real garments and placing them in Augmented Reality. We realized that Augmented Reality technology makes it possible for users to place virtual clothing anywhere, at any time, leading to expanded brand recognition.

Cutting edge technology, such as AR and 3D, can help us to radically change the status quo of the fashion industry, benefitting both the partner-brands and end consumers.

We are pleased to invite RESERVED to be part of our beta platform. After extensive research, we concluded that you and your incredible team can benefit from the features inside of YZED.

RESERVED could benefit from having full digitally built clothing pieces and elevated brand impact. The new format, will allow you to showcase your products with incredible amounts of detailing, which could result in higher conversion rates. YZED can assist in translating your garments into 3D, placing them in AR environment, and building the web architecture around it. We are a team of highly-skilled professionals, specializing in 3D design, graphic design, UI/UX, programming and business consultancy. We are comfortable working with tools such as Spark AR, Cinema4D, Clo3D, Adobe CC, React.js and more.
This page at yzed.me was prototyped specially for your brand. We created a 3D model and 3D animation based on RESERVED’s ECO AWARE collection. For better experience, we kindly advise you to view the detailed 3D assets on your computer and the AR can be accessed through your mobile device.

YZED is a 3D & AR creative effort idealized by Batyrkhan Sanashkin, Tiago de Sousa, Renée Neggers and Theran Brigowatz. Our international team is a part of  “101Global”, which is a Seoul-based innovation hub.

By working with YZED, RESERVED could also use 101Global’s ecosystem to expand physically in Seoul. We currently own two spaces: a pop-up store at the renowned Galleria Department Store in Apgujeong (G.STREET101), and a shared workspace building in Gangnam district (Space101).

In conclusion, if you are interested in our beliefs and digital fashion services in 3D and AR, please reply to this email. We can’t wait to hear from you soon!

Hopefully, together we can help the world to discover self-expression through fashion.
